HTML:HyperText MarkupLanguage,html 5
>used to make web pages
>not a programming language, markup langugage
>text and images, but markup describing the formatting you want. For example, bold text,tables 
<html>....contains all elements
metadata tags:
<head>......info about page title,scripts
<title>.....page title
sectioning tags:
<body>.....contains all items seen on page
<h1......section header,<h2>,<h3>....

<p>.....paragraph
<div>.....defines section of web page

<b>......text stylistically different-bold
<em>......emphasize text(slanted text)

images and multimedia tags:
<img src=" link"/>...width also reduced.
<a href=" link">for tagging a link</a>(for opening other website)

simple lists:
<ul>.......unordrerd(inside ul must have sequence of li elements)
<li>.......list orderd
<ol>.....orderd list(inside ol must have sequence of li elements,like 1,2,3,.....A,B,C...)

tables:
info in rows and columns
<table>
<tr>........rows
<th>.......header elements in row
<td>......table cells
